Camera Permission

The app requires camera access to allow users to capture photos. Photos are captured only when the user presses the capture button. The app does not access the camera in the background.

Location (GPS) Permission

The app uses location (GPS) access to:

  • Fetch current latitude and longitude
  • Display address and location name
  • Stamp GPS details, date, and time on photos

Location data is used only while the app is open and is processed locally on the device. The app does not store, track, or share location data.

Photos and Storage

All photos captured using the app are saved directly to the user's device gallery. The app does not upload photos to any server or cloud service.

Data Collection and Sharing

The app does not collect, store, sell, or share personal or sensitive user data.

Specifically, the app does not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Contacts
  • Messages or call logs
  • Device identifiers
  • Background location data
